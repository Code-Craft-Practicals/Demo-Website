About Us

Our mission and vision

CODE CRAFT is dedicated to empowering individuals with the skills and knowledge required to excel in the field of web development. Our mission is to create a better future through education and innovation.

Founded in 2024, we aim to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving tech industry by providing practical, hands-on learning experiences.

We also offer online lectures and interactive sessions, making it easier for students to learn from anywhere in the world.

Our Activities

Snapshots from our online lectures and hands-on workshops

Group Discussions

Teams worked together to build knowledge and gain insights from classmates, helping to develop and improve connections between them.

This activity helps develop teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills among participants.

Buffering the Brain

Giving sheets of paper to write the first five words that come to mind when seeing five different black and white pictures.

This activity encourages creative thinking and helps participants express their immediate thoughts visually.

Personal Skills

Students enrolled with us not only get the oppurtunity to craft by coding but also get an oppurtunity to craft themselves.

This activity focuses on personal growth, enhancing skills like time management, communication, and leadership.

 Exploring World Out of the Code

Interactive sessions where students were given topics to explain and study on it (Best part: Topics were out of the syllabus)

This activity broadens students' horizons, encouraging them to explore topics beyond their usual curriculum.

Let the WAR Begin

Two teams—Girls and Boys—competed in a friendly debate, presenting valid points about the OSI Model. The lively exchange encouraged everyone to participate and think critically. (Curious about the winner? Reach out to us below!)

This activity promotes healthy competition, teamwork, and deeper understanding of technical concepts in a fun, engaging way.
