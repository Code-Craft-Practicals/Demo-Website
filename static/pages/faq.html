    Code Craft is a student-led initiative dedicated to teach Coding, Web Development, and Digital Skills through interactive Workshops, Online Classes, and hands-on activities for learners of all levels.

    Our mission is to empower students with practical coding skills, foster creativity, and build a strong foundation for future careers in technology.
