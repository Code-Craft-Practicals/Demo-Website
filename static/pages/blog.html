Workshop 1: Web Development Fundamentals – Rozario High School

By Team CODE CRAFT | March 2025

  • Rozario High School
  • 4-Day Intensive
  • SDLC, HTML, CSS, Personality Development

Code Craft conducted a 4-day intensive workshop at Rozario High School, focused on introducing students to the world of web development. Each day spanned 5 to 6 hours of hands-on learning. We introduced key concepts such as SDLC (Software Development Life Cycle), HTML, CSS, and the basics of webpage styling.

Alongside technical education, students were given a brief introduction to Python and engaged in sessions on personality development and critical thinking. Interactive activities, online quizzes, and a final certification helped enhance learning. It wasn't just about coding — it was about confidence-building and holistic growth.

Workshop 2: Introduction to Web Technologies – Om Computer Classes

By Team CODE CRAFT | April 2025

  • Om Computer Institute
  • 7-Day Comprehensive
  • HTML, C++, Python Basics

Our 7-day workshop at Om Computer Institute was designed to give students a solid foundation in web development and programming. Each day included a 2-hour session conducted in two shifts to accommodate more learners.

Students explored the basics of HTML, and got hands-on with simple C++ and Python programs. The workshop helped them understand how software is developed, and also gave them their first taste of programming logic and structure. It was the perfect blend of theory and practice, made easy for beginners.

Workshop 3: Hello World!!! – R.K. Classes

By Team CODE CRAFT | April - May 2025

  • R.K. Classes
  • Weekly Sessions for 2 Months
  • Web Design, Programming, Graphics, Networking Basics

"Hello World!!!" – the iconic first step for every programmer — became the title and spirit of our long-term workshop at R.K. Classes. For 2 months, we conducted weekly sessions that introduced students to HTML, CSS, and basic programming languages.

We also touched on software concepts, graphic designing, and networking fundamentals, offering students a well-rounded tech foundation. To make learning fun and impactful, we incorporated games and memory-based activities that enhanced critical thinking and retention power. A perfect mix of learning and fun!

Behind the Scenes – How We Plan a Workshop

A look at our process and teamwork

At Code Craft, every workshop starts with one thing: a spark of purpose. Whether it's introducing kids to coding or sharpening someone's logic-building skills, we plan with a mission in mind.

Topic Research: We brainstorm topics that are relevant, exciting, and beginner-friendly.
Content Creation: From presentations to coding exercises, our trainers design material that blends theory with practice.
Role Division: Every team member has a part to play – trainers, coordinators, designers, content creators, and technical support.
Tool Time: We use tools like Canva for graphics, PowerPoint for slides, Git for version control, and lots of custom coding examples.
Dry Run: Before every workshop, we do a full internal trial – just to make sure things run smooth.
"Planning a workshop is like crafting a mini tech festival – fun, structured, and full of learning."
