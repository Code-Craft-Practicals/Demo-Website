Our Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us at CODE CRAFT

At CODE CRAFT, your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you share when you browse our website, join our workshops, or interact with us online.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any other details you provide during workshop registration, contact forms, or feedback submissions.
  • Usage Data: Information like pages visited, time spent on the site, and browser/device details collected through cookies or analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To manage and improve our workshops, services, and website experience.
  • To send you workshop updates, confirmations, certificates, and support.
  • To showcase student work (e.g., name/photo) for educational or promotional purposes, with proper care.
  • To analyze website behavior and enhance content quality.

3. Data Sharing & Security

  • We do not sell or misuse your personal data.
  • We may share limited data only with trusted service providers (like email platforms or analytics tools) necessary for operations.
  • We take all reasonable steps to secure your data and prevent unauthorized access.

4. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies and tools like Google Analytics to improve your experience and understand how visitors interact with our site.
You can manage cookie settings in your browser at any time.

5. Your Rights

  • You may request access, update, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting us.
  • You can unsubscribe from communications by using the link provided in our emails.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices.
Any updates will be posted here with a revised date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, feel free to contact us via our official page or email: codecraftoffice01@gmail.com
